ABOUT US

We are passionate about serving high-quality coffee to our customers. Our coffee beans are sourced from the best coffee farms around the world.

At KATEAPS, our journey began in 2024 with a small coffee shop in the heart of the city. Over the years, we have grown into a beloved destination for coffee enthusiasts, known for our commitment to excellence and sustainability. Quality is at the forefront of everything we do. From our meticulously selected beans to our expertly crafted drinks, we strive to deliver an unforgettable coffee experience with every cup. Our team consists of dedicated baristas who are passionate about their craft and are always eager to share their knowledge and love for coffee. We take pride in our friendly and attentive service, ensuring that every customer feels valued and appreciated.
